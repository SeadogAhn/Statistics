The data sets are provided in ASCII, Minitab 
JMP-IN, and Excel file formats.  

File names follow the conventions in the book:
 'ex' for exercise, 'exp' for example followed by the 
chapter number and then the problem number.  
Thus, exp6-3.xls is example 3 of chapter 6.
